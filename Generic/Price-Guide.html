Boiler and Pressure Vessel (BPV) Certifications

New/Renewal
H (Cast Iron and/or Cast Aluminum) PRT $1,175.00 per certificate
$2,590.00 per certificate
All other Certificates $3,525.00 per certificate
Certification Mark 340.00 per stamp plus applicable tax and shipping
Note: The Certification Mark fee only applies to new Certificate Holders or Certificate Holders who would like additional marks.
Advance Deposit $11,000.00

Note: Where ASME appoints an ASME designee to conduct the review, ASME requires an advance review deposit (which will be applied to the cost of the review) in addition to certificate and Certification Mark fees. The review deposit is required for the following:

  • All H (Cast Iron and/or Cast Aluminum) and RP reviews.
  • All reviews where the jurisdiction (state or province) is the Authorized Inspection Agency.
  • Reviews in states and territories where ASME conducts the review (Idaho, Wyoming, Puerto Rico, and the Virgin Islands).
  • A review deposit to ASME is not required for V, HV, UV, UV3, UD, TV, and TD certificates.

Additional Locations

Application of Certificates at more than one location $6,000.00 per location added to deposit
2 Year Temporary Location Authorization (BPV) $2,850.00

Additional Information: Company Name Change or Plant Location Change at $250.00 each occurrence

General Notes:

  • Prices are based on USD and are subject to change. If you have any questions, please contact the ASME Conformity Assessment Department at ca@asme.org
  • It is required that renewal applications, fees, and review deposit be received by ASME at least four to six months prior to the expiration date of current certificate(s).
  • Cancellation of survey dates and withdrawal of applications is subject to a monetary penalty.
  • Advance deposits do not cover certificate fees.
