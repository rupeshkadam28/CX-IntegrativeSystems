How do you know what you’re worth?

Whether you’re negotiating your salary or planning for a raise, knowing your professional value can help you make sure you’re fairly compensated for the work you do.

ASME Salary Services consist of an annual survey that compiles comprehensive data from thousands of engineering professionals to create salary calculators tailored to your personal information.

Two different kind of calculators are offered:

  • The Regression Salary Calculator uses regression analysis, allowing the user to calculate individual compensation results for thousands of possible employment situations. The regression model makes compensation adjustments based on the criteria you select.
  • The Searchable Survey Data Tool allows you to create customized aggregations of the survey results. This tool is based on the survey data (and sample sizes) and thus is better suited for less specific situations.
