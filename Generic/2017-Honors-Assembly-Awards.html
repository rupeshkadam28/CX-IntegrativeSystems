Each year, the ASME Committee on Honors confers awards to over 65 individuals, recognizing meritorious contributions to engineering. The process begins with screenings by the General Awards Committee and a series of special award committees, with approval by the Committee on Honors.

The recipient of the ASME Medal and those awarded Honorary Membership are recognized annually at the Honors Assembly, an evening gala held during ASME’s International Mechanical Engineering Congress and Exposition (IMECE). The Committee on Honors selects several other award recipients for recognition at the Honors Assembly as well.

The Honors Assembly is a joyous event each November as ASME leaders, volunteers, staff and other IMECE attendees join together to learn about and applaud the accomplishments of distinguished individuals. Here we present video biographies of the eight 2017 Honors Assembly awardees.
