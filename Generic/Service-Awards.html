Dedicated Service Award
Award honors dedicated voluntary service to the Society marked by outstanding performance, demonstrated effective leadership, prolonged and committed service, devotion, enthusiasm and faithfulness.

Award is presented to a current/former Outstanding Student Section Advisor whose leadership and service qualities have contributed to the program and operations of a Student Section of the Society.
