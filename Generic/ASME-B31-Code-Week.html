ASME B31 CODE WEEK MASTERCLASSES

MasterClasses at ASME Code Weeks provide the unique opportunity to learn directly from the ASME Code experts discussing the latest developments and real world applications related to ASME Pressure Vessel and Piping Codes. This event also offers anopportunity for course attendees to participate in the Code Week committee meetings and network with the experts in the industry.

For inquiries about the program or other MasterClass programs, contact Jennifer Delda, Program Manager, at 212-591-7108 or deldaj@asme.org

SCHEDULE IS NOW AVAILABLE

Join us in Cincinnati, OH for the ASME B31 and Nonmetallic Pressure Piping Code Week & Master Classes to be held at the Hilton Cincinnati Netherland Plaza from September 17 – 21, 2018.

The B31 Pressure Piping and Nonmetallic Committee Code Week is a forum for business leaders and industry experts to discuss high profile topics related to the ASME B31 and Nonmetallic Codes concerning the design, manufacture, fabrication, test, installation, and operation of pressure piping systems. The ASME B31 and Nonmetallic Committee Code Week assembles committee meetings, bringing together high-level industry experts from all over the world. Attended by industry professionals, participation includes representation from government agencies, multinational corporations, plant owners, utilities, pressure equipment fabricators, designers, materials suppliers and others in related industries.

Code Week committee meetings are open to the public. Participants are requested to register in advance.
