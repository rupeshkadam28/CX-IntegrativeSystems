Registration

All Code Week participants and Workshop attendees are requested to register in advance in order to be provided with name badges onsite. Advance registration helps ASME to manage the conference more efficiently, plan for future Code Week meetings, as well as identify potential new committee members.

Registration to attend the Code Week committee meetings is free. Committee participants are requested to wear their badges in the meetings. When you get to Code Week, stop by the ASME Office/Registration Desk to pick up your name badge.

ASME Travel Policy: ASME is not responsible for the purchase of non-refundable airline tickets or the cancellation/change fees associated with canceling a flight. Please call to confirm that the event is running before purchasing airline tickets. ASME retains the right to cancel an event up to 2 weeks before scheduled presentation date.
