ASME Capitol Update is published weekly on the ASME website and as an electronic newsletter focused on legislative and regulatory news of interest to the engineering community. Capitol Update provides valuable insight on the latest public policy developments from Capitol Hill on key issues such as energy, research and development, education, environment, standards, and other issues.

The articles contained in Capitol Update are not positions of ASME or any of its subentities, unless specifically noted as such. This publication is designed to inform ASME members about issues of concern being debated and discussed in the halls of congress, in the states, and in the federal agencies.

To subscribe, please visit here or send your request to GRdept@asme.org
